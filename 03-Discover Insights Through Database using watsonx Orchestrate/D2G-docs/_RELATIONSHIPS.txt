# D2G Database — Table Relationships & Subject Areas

## Overview
The D2G database is a **multi-subject-area analytical database** covering Sales, Customer Service, Finance, HR/Workforce, Procurement, IT/BI Audit, and Sales Pipeline. It follows a **star schema** pattern with shared dimension tables.

---

## Shared Dimension Tables

| Dimension | Key Column | Used By Fact Tables |
|---|---|---|
| [`Time`](Time.md) | `Date_ID` | Call_Center_Fact, Customer_Service_Fact, CustomerLoyalty, IT_Fact, Order_Fact, Pipeline_Fact, Procurement_Fact, Recruiting_Fact, Training_Fact |
| [`HR_Calendar`](HR_Calendar.md) | `Date_ID` | HR_Fact, Workforce_Fact |
| [`Fiscal_Period`](Fiscal_Period.md) | `Date_ID` | Finance_Fact |
| [`Customer`](Customer.md) | `Customer_ID` | Call_Center_Fact, Customer_Service_Fact, CustomerLoyalty, Finance_Fact, Order_Fact, Pipeline_Fact |
| [`Products`](Products.md) | `Product_ID` | Call_Center_Fact, Customer_Service_Fact, CustomerLoyalty, Order_Fact, Pipeline_Fact, Procurement_Fact |
| [`Employee`](Employee.md) | `Employee_ID` | HR_Fact, Workforce_Fact, Attrition_Study, Recruiting_Fact, Training_Fact |
| [`Geography`](Geography.md) | `Sales_ID` | Customer_Service_Fact, CustomerLoyalty, Order_Fact, Pipeline_Fact |
| [`Organization`](Organization.md) | `Org_ID` | Finance_Fact, Procurement_Fact |
| [`Vendor`](Vendor.md) | `Vendor_ID` | Finance_Fact, Procurement_Fact |
| [`Channel`](Channel.md) | `Channel_ID` | CustomerLoyalty, Order_Fact |

## Key Observations

1. **Three separate time dimensions** coexist:
   - `Time` — daily calendar (2014+), for operational facts
   - `HR_Calendar` — monthly calendar (2009+), for HR facts
   - `Fiscal_Period` — fiscal periods (FY09+), for finance facts

2. **Geography doubles as a Sales Rep dimension** — `Sales_ID` maps to a sales rep + territory, not just a location.

3. **CustomerLoyalty is a pre-joined analytical table** — it embeds Customer and Product attributes directly, making it faster for self-service analytics but denormalized.

4. **Attrition_Study is a wide analytical extract** — combines HR, training, and fleet data in one row; not a normalized fact table.

5. **BI Audit tables (Run_Jobs, Run_Reports, View_Reports, User_Logins)** are standalone logs from the Cognos BI platform — they have no joins to business fact tables.

6. **Fleet_Operations is empty** — may be a placeholder for future data.
